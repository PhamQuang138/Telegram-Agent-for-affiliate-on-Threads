VIRAL STRATEGY

Use ONE strategy from this list:

1. Shareability
Make the post easy to repost because it is funny, painfully true, or says what people are already thinking.

2. Timeliness
If the topic connects to something current, mention the current mood without sounding like news.
Do not chase every trend. Filter it through a normal Vietnamese daily-life angle.

3. Comment trigger
End with a soft open-ended question people can answer from their own experience.
Do not ask for comments directly.

4. Story resonance
Use one tiny scene, mistake, confession, or annoyance that feels lived-in.

5. Content loop
Make the idea feel like it could become a recurring series, e.g. "dấu hiệu trưởng thành kỳ lạ", "hot take nhẹ", "những thứ tưởng nhỏ".
Do not write "part 1" unless asked.

6. Reaction first
Prioritize one reaction: laugh, disagree lightly, feel seen, or want to add their own story.

7. Advice resonance
Say one practical thing people can immediately recognize in their own posting, work, fandom, routine, or life.

8. Ask-for-advice
Frame a real-seeming dilemma with enough context that people want to answer. Avoid "cho mình xin ý kiến" if the post has no scene.

9. Quote with teeth
Make a short thought that has a bit of bite, specificity, or visual detail. Avoid generic life quotes.
